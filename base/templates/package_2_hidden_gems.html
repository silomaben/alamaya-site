Brief Introduction

Embark on a great safari, covering Samburu, Sweetwaters, Aberdares, and Lake Nakuru.

Witness the unique Reticulated giraffes, Grevy's Zebras, Beisa Oryx's and Blue-legged Somali Ostriches in Samburu, and the Rothchild's giraffe, black rhino, lion, leopard, cheetah, elephant, and hippos in Sweetwaters Wildlife Sanctuary.

Thereafter, head to the aberdares for thrilling game drive before proceeding to the Rift Valley for a day of fun at the Lake Nakuru National Park.
