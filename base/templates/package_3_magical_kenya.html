Brief Introduction

Start with a thrilling hot air balloon ride in the Maasai Mara, if you please, and be a guest to Nakuru's pink flamingo land.

This trip blows the essence of natural beaty right through your soul with breathtaking views of Lake Bogoria and Lake Naivasha.

Visits to the Giraffe Centre and Elephant Orphanage allow you to appreaciate nature's beauty from a close view, while Amboseli park's elephant herds and Mt. Kilimanjaro pose for your excited camera.

Save your goosebumps for Tsavo West's "Shetani" lava flows and Chyulu Hills, and then drop your safari haste by a relaxing poolside at coast(Diani)
